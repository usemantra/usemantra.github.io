01 Vision

Our vision is to shape a world where materials are part of an ever flowing ethereal fabric that manifests into physicality as needed. This hyper-object of all materiality grows, spikes and vanishes like waves, it transforms into uses and travels between realities and physicalities. This vision is unbound to humanities supply-chain and moves beyond space and time. We are dreaming of a fully circular system that leave not a single atom behind and constructs new use out of all the unused.

Mantra facilitates a mode of production that is friction less, safe, traceable, adaptable, instant and carbon neutral. Our tools are made to reuse materials and bring them back into circularity. Producers can tap into an suite of solutions that offers them sourcing services, warehouses become fluid places and they can use them for active purposes.

Mantra aims to become the definitive place for managing carbon neutral circular supply-chains.
